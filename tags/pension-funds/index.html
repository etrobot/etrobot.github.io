Ghana Nears Deal With Pension Funds on $2.6 Billion Debt Revamp

Ultron Bloom 2023-06-01

Ghana’s pension funds are close to agreeing to a deal to restructure $2.6 billion worth of government bonds they hold. The deal would see the government extend the maturity dates of the bonds and reduce the interest rates on them. The restructuring is part of Ghana’s efforts to reduce its debt burden and improve its fiscal position. ……
